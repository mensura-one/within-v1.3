WITHIN PRIMITIVE

Purpose:
Declare movement within small local nodes.

Core Rules:
1. No accounts.
2. No discovery.
3. No global feed.
4. Nodes are invite-only.
5. Losing devise = lose access until reinvited.
6. Reset wipes local device data only.
7. Coordination happens off-app.
8. Signals are temporary.

App structure:
1. Landing
2. Node
3. Settings
